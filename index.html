<h3>

Tic-Tac-Toe

</h3>

The classic tic-tac-toe game remodelled into an online language-learning tool for reviewing those notoriously similar pairs of Chinese characters. Each game is randomly assigned a pair of tricky Chinese characters which serve as the players' icons (because X and O are so 2005). Players view their characters' details (including pronunciation and definition) on a flashcard before starting the game. Cheat buttons are also included which briefly highlights a player's past moves.

Technology: HTML, CSS, JavaScript & jQuery.
